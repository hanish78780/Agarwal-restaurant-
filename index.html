About Agrawal Restaurant

Experience the finest Indian cuisine in the heart of Jaipur

Our Story

Founded by Hanish Singhal, Agrawal Restaurant has been serving the finest Indian cuisine for years. We combine traditional recipes with contemporary presentation, creating an unforgettable culinary journey for our guests.

Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and exceptional service has made us one of Jaipur's most beloved dining destinations.
